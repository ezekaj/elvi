## 1. Sofia Hotel AI (2025)

**Category:** Voice AI for Hospitality
**Status:** Production
**Industry:** Hotel & Hospitality

### Overview
Sofia is an advanced voice AI receptionist system designed specifically for the hospitality industry. The system handles guest inquiries, checks room availability in real-time, qualifies leads, and transfers qualified prospects to human booking managers.

### Technical Stack
- **Voice Processing:** Twilio (telephony integration)
- **Speech-to-Text:** Deepgram / OpenAI Whisper
- **AI Engine:** OpenAI GPT-4
- **Text-to-Speech:** ElevenLabs / OpenAI TTS
- **Backend:** Python
- **Real-time Communication:** WebSockets, WebRTC

### Key Features
1. **Natural Conversation:** Multi-turn dialogue handling with context awareness
2. **Real-time Availability:** Checks hotel room availability with <500ms response time
3. **Lead Qualification:** Collects guest information (dates, room preferences, contact details)
4. **Intelligent Transfer:** Connects qualified leads to human booking managers via Twilio
5. **Multi-language Support:** English and Albanian (expandable)
6. **24/7 Availability:** Handles inquiries outside business hours

### Architecture
- **Voice AI does NOT create bookings** (human-in-the-loop for financial transactions)
- **Lead filtering:** Only serious inquiries reach booking managers
- **Data privacy:** Guest data stored only during active calls
- **Fallback system:** Escalates to human when needed

### Performance Metrics
- **Lead Qualification Rate:** 70%+ (target)
- **Response Time:** <300ms average
- **Availability:** 99.9% uptime
- **Customer Satisfaction:** High (reduces wait times)

### Business Impact
- Reduces workload on hotel staff
- Captures leads 24/7
- Filters out non-serious inquiries
- Improves booking manager efficiency
- Scalable to multiple hotel properties
